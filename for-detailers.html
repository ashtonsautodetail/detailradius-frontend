Questions detailers ask

What does it cost to join?

Nothing. It's free to create your listing and there's no monthly fee. DetailRadius only takes a 10% fee on jobs you actually complete through the platform.

How and when do I get paid?

Customers pay through DetailRadius — a deposit to secure the booking and the balance when the work's done. When you mark a job complete, your share (90%) transfers to your connected bank account.

Do I need my own website or booking software?

No. Your DetailRadius profile is your booking page, and the dashboard is your business software — calendar, customers, analytics, reviews and payouts all in one place.

Mobile, drop-off, or both?

Your choice. Set your service area and offer mobile service, drop-off, or both. Customers see exactly what you offer.

Can I set my own prices?

Absolutely. You define every service and price, and you can adjust the final price on a job before the customer pays the balance.
